About LC
In large retailer transactions, trust is crucial between buyers and suppliers. Funds are typically transferred before shipment, but in some cases, it may happen after delivery, especially in B2B transactions. However, enforcing legal agreements across borders can be challenging, putting parties at risk of losing funds or goods. To address this, financial institutions provide a solution called a "Letter of Credit" that facilitates secure exchanges, protecting both parties involved.
Objectives
Automate and digitize the LC issuance, amendment, and settlement process.
